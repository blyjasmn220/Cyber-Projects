Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is the recipe to a delicious chocolate chip cookie that will rock your world.

If your want your taste buds to be rocked, here it finally the recipe for a rocked taste bud.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (2 1/4)
  • Baking soda (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Unsalted butter (1 cup)
  • Granulated white sugar (3/4 cup)
  • Brown sugar (3/4 cup)
  • Eggs (2 large)
  • Vanilla extract (1 to 2 teaspoons)
  • Chocolate chips (2 cups)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. Place the dry mixture away from the prep area
  4. Place the soften butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar in large bowl
  5. Beat until smooth, fluffy, and light in color
  6. Mix in eggs one at a time
  7. Stir in the vanilla extract until fully incorporated
  8. Gradually pour the dry flour mixture into the wet ingredients
  9. Stir on low speed just until flour disappears
  10. Gently stir in the chocolate chips into the mixture
  11. Place cookies in the oven for about 9-11 minutes until golden brown
